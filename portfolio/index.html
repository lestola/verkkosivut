About Us

The Order, is a Finnish group of hardstyle producers. Members of The Order family are Ville "Willhard" Lehtola, Otto "Seamlezz" Palmu and Marko "Dj Boggy-B" Lehtola. The Order started their journey in the year 2013 and now they are chasing their dream of becoming a huge name in the scene.

The Order is known from their unique sound and melodies and total dedication for the harder styles. Sacrificing almost everything, spending countless hours in the studio, true passion towards music is what makes them, who they are. Be prepared, they will rise and freeze your souls.
